Technologies and Contract Research Services

We provide a comprehensive portfolio of innovative technologies and tailored contract research services.

Our technology offerings include ready-to-transfer processes for Acarbose, Astaxanthin, Compactin, Lovastatin, and Pravastatin. We specialize in custom monoclonal and polyclonal antibody development and production, as well as recombinant protein expression and purification. Additionally, we offer contract process development for cGMP manufacturing, including expertise in fermentation and downstream purification.

For more information about the technologies and services, please send us an email to sales@jiagen.com.
